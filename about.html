About Playboi Carti's "I AM MUSIC" Album

"I AM MUSIC" is Playboi Carti's highly anticipated fourth studio album, released in 2024. The album showcases Carti's signature style, blending trap beats with experimental sounds, and features collaborations with Kanye West, Travis Scott, Ken Carson and Desrtroy Lonely.

Album Concept

The album explores themes of love and offers a unique perspective on grief.
